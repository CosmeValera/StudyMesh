# Derivatives

## Core Definition

Definition:: A derivative measures instantaneous rate of change.

Definition:: Graphically, a derivative represents the slope of the tangent line.

## Important Rules

Flashcard::
Q: What is the power rule?
A: d/dx x^n = n*x^(n-1)

Flashcard::
Q: What happens to constants when differentiating?
A: Constants become 0.

Flashcard::
Q: What is the derivative of x?
A: 1

## Worked Examples

Example::
Problem: Find the derivative of f(x) = 3x^4 - 2x^2 + 7
Solution: f'(x) = 12x^3 - 4x

Example::
Problem: Find the derivative of y = x^5 + 2x^2 - 9
Solution: y' = 5x^4 + 4x

## Practice Questions

Quiz::
Q: Find the derivative of y = x^3 + 5x^2 - 8x + 4
A: 3x^2 + 10x - 8

Quiz::
Q: Differentiate y = 7x^2 + 3
A: 14x

Quiz::
Q: Differentiate y = 2x^6
A: 12x^5

## Common Mistakes

- Forgetting to reduce the exponent by 1
- Forgetting constants disappear
- Multiplying incorrectly after applying the power rule
