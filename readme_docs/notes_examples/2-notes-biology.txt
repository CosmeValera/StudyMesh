bio — cell stuff

prokaryotes = no nucleus, smaller, bacteria. eukaryotes have nucleus + organelles, plants/animals/fungi etc. mitochondria = makes ATP (energy currency, remember ATP = adenosine triphosphate). chloroplasts only plants + algae? photosynthesis there.

cell membrane is phospholipid bilayer, hydrophilic heads out, hydrophobic tails in. proteins floating around = “fluid mosaic” thing. probably important.

diffusion goes high to low concentration. osmosis = water moving across membrane, also high water to low water? teacher said think of salty side pulling water in. review this bc I always flip it.

rough ER has ribosomes, smooth ER no ribosomes, Golgi edits/packages/sends. lysosomes break junk down.

mitosis order: prophase, metaphase, anaphase, telophase. PMAT. chromosomes line middle in meta, pull apart in ana.
